Animal Guides

Whale Sharks

The whale shark is the largest fish on earth, measuring up to 40 feet long. Despite its size, it only feeds on plankton and small fish and squid. Georgia Aquarium is the only aquarium outside of Asia to house these amazing animals.

Asian Small Clawed Otter

The Asian small-clawed otter is the smallest otter in the world and is known for its energetic nature. It spends more time on land than any other otter and can communicate with other otters using 12 different calls
